1. Pierre Hermé – “Picasso of the pastry world”

It is no coincidence that people nicknamed Pierre Hermé "Picasso of the pastry world" , Pierre Hermé's top-notch baking skills made the village of Cake Chefs nod in recognition. Moreover, Pierre Hermé's way of creating flavors is also very rich and unusual, which is why he is associated with the name "Picasso of the pastry world". Pierre Hermé is the youngest person to win the title of Best Pastry Chef of the year in France. Currently, he is managing 2 Michelin star restaurants in France.

2. François Payard – "Famous Baker”

With talent from skillful hands, outstanding creativity and passion for cakes, François Payard has prepared many luxurious desserts, satisfying even the most demanding guests. Not only a pastry expert, François Payard is also a great businessman when he owns many successful pastry shops around the world.

3. Christina Tosi– “Queen” of Cakes

If you've seen the show Masterchef season 6, it will probably be no stranger to the name of Christina Tosi - who has replaced the tough judge Joe Bastianich to lead in the powerful trio with Gordon Ramsay and Graham Elliot. Christina Tosi is also known as the world-famous female American Chef, she specializes in pastries that make people unable to take their eyes off of those wonderful works. Many people think that Christina Tosi was probably born to be a Cake Chef, is this completely true? Let's explore with Chefjob the path to success of "queen" of pastry Christina Tosi.

Passion for cakes has motivated Christina Tosi to boldly embark on a professional path after completing professional training courses. She founded Momofuku Milk Bar - a daily bakery for 6 retail stores in New York and one in Toronto, Canada. For Christina Tosi, Milk Bar is not only a place for her to satisfy her passion for cakes or assert herself in the business field, but also a place for Tosi to fulfill her desire to change the "face" of pastry in cuisine. And the first "sweet fruits" came, Milk Bar entered the Top of America's most interesting bakeries voted by Bon Appetit magazine. Christina Tosi is also the author of two cookbooks, Momofuku Milk Bar and Milk Bar life, which are loved by both industry chefs and non-believers. Currently, Tosi is the manager of more than 200 employees working at her stores, proving her position in the culinary industry is not small at all. The top publications and programs that Christina Tosi participates in are warmly welcomed by the audience, including Masterchef America, Christina Tosi has replaced the difficult Joe Bastianich to lead in the powerful trio with Gordon Ramsay and Graham Elliot.
