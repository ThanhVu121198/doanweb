Pierre Hermé – “Picasso of the pastry world”

France’s Pastry King: Pierre Hermé reveals chocolate's secrets

It is no coincidence that people nicknamed Pierre Hermé "Picasso of the pastry world" , Pierre Hermé's top-notch baking skills made the village of Cake Chefs nod in recognition. Moreover, Pierre Hermé's way of creating flavors is also very rich and unusual, which is why he is associated with the name "Picasso of the pastry world". Pierre Hermé is the youngest person to win the title of Best Pastry Chef of the year in France. Currently, he is managing 2 Michelin star restaurants in France.
