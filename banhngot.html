Anh Hoa Bakery is a French pastry brand of Anh Hoa Bakery Joint Stock Company.
Established in 2004 at Ngo Tram street, Hoan Kiem district, Hanoi. Over 15
years of development, until now, Anh Hoa Bakery has 13 business establishments
located on densely populated streets in Hanoi. Anh Hoa Bakery products are made
from imported ingredients from countries with traditional baking such as Newzeland
, USA, France, Belgium. With delicious flavors typical of cream, butter, milk,
cheese, almonds, chocolate... under the skillful hands of experienced bakers.
The factory scale is more than 2000m2 with advanced and modern equipment according
to ISO 2018 standards, the whole factory is covered with special EPOXY paint.
Hoa Bakery always brings customers the best quality products, absolutely guaranteed
on food safety and hygiene.
